Welcome to the scene evolution survey!

Thank you for agreeing to take part in this survey!
The survey contains 9 tasks, each of which contains 3 marking queries, and will take about 30 minutes to finish.

Each of the following task cosists of an action sequence, an initial 3D scene (top right), and 3 scene evolutions to reflect the given action sequence and initial scene.
One evolution was manually generated by a human, and the other two are synthesized by a computer algorithm.

Please score each scene evolution in the range of 1 to 5, based on following two criteria:
1) Plausibility: the scene evolution is a plausible result after the given action sequence is performed on the initial scene.
2) Naturalness: the scene looks natural.

Scoring guidance:
1 = "Completely random, implausible scene"
3 = "Somewhat plausible scene"
5 = "Very plausible scene"
