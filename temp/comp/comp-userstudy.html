Welcome to the scene layout survey!

Thank you for agreeing to take part in this survey!
The survey contains 6 tasks, each of which contains 5 marking queries, and will take about 15 minutes to finish.

Each of the following task cosists of an action description (the title), an initial 3D scene (the top left with black border), and 5 more scenes to reflect the given action and initial scene.
Those scenes were either manually generated by a human, or synthesized by various computer algorithms.

Please score each scene in the range of 1 to 5, based on following two criteria:
1) Plausibility: the scene is a plausible result after the given action is performed on the initial scene.
2) Naturalness: the scene looks natural.

Scoring guidance:
1 = "Completely random, implausible scene"
3 = "Somewhat plausible scene"
5 = "Very plausible scene"
Please DO focus on the object layouts regarding to the given actions.
